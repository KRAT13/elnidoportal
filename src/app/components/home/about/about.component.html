About Me

Ryan G. Sanchez

Fullstack Web Developer

Brgy. Buena Suerte, El Nido, Palawan, Philippines

"As a proud native of El Nido, Palawan, I created this responsive website to share the unmatched beauty of my beloved hometown with the world. More than just a showcase, this platform aims to make local information more accessible—through a user-friendly, ad-free experience designed with our community in mind.

This project also serves as a meaningful addition to my portfolio as a fullstack web developer. But beyond that, it is my way of giving back—by offering a digital space that highlights what El Nido has to offer, connects locals and visitors alike, and supports the town in small yet impactful ways.

Welcome, and I hope this site becomes a helpful and inspiring resource for you."
